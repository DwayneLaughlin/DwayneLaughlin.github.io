About

I attended Loyola University Chicago, graduating in summer of 2011 with a BA in Music. Since graduation I have spent time working as a music teacher, performing pianist, composer and producer. For the last 4 years I have also been the current lead for the account management/collections team for zZounds.com, a leading company in the instrument retail business. Part of my day to day job is managing training content in our internal wiki page and doing coding in HTML. I have experience with several coding languages including HTML, SQL, and JavaScript

I am a 2020 gradutate of the Full Stack Coding Bootcamp at Northwestern University. This portfolio was specifically designed with HTML and CSS.
